Brain Stroke Prevention Guide

🥗 Dietary Guidelines

  • Reduce Salt: Limit daily sodium intake to less than 5g (1 tsp).
  • Potassium Rich: Increase intake of bananas, spinach, and sweet potatoes.
  • Avoid Trans-fats: Stay away from packaged snacks and fried foods.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily.

🏃 Physical Activity

  • Brisk Walking: Aim for 30 minutes of moderate cardio daily.
  • Strength Training: Include muscle-strengthening twice a week.
  • Stress Control: 15 mins of Yoga or Deep Breathing (Pranayama).
  • Sleep Hygiene: Ensure 7-8 hours of quality restorative sleep.

*These guidelines are based on WHO and AHA standards for cardiovascular health.
